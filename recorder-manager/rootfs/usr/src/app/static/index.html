First-Time Setup

To let this add-on manage your recorder filters, add these two lines inside your recorder: block in configuration.yaml:

recorder:
  include: !include recorder_include.yaml
  exclude: !include recorder_exclude.yaml
If you already have a recorder: block, just add the two !include lines inside it. Your existing settings like purge_keep_days remain under your control and are not touched by this add-on.
